Bill Pay

The City of Shawnee has an online website in cooperation with the ABC company. You can have accounts to pay for municipal authority utility, and court citations. The website provides a simple way to pay all our utility city-related information into one easy manage platform

Service Fee:

Effective March 1, 2026 , a 10.0% service fee will be assessed to your credit or debit card payments on the account.

eCheck transactions will incur a service fee of $1.95 (checks cannot be accepted over the telephone).

    Click to continue to Online Account Access

    You can pay with VISA or Master Card online and view your Utility Payments and Usage History.

    Residents can also choose from several convenient payment options. In addition to online payments, the City of Shawnee accepts payments in person, by mail, or at authorized kiosks located throughout the community.

    Residents are encouraged to select the payment method that works best for their needs. Online payments offer the fastest processing time, while in-person and mail-in options remain available for those who prefer traditional methods.

  • Cash Payment in Person.
  • Check Payment in Person or by Mail.
  • Kiosks at Tag agency.

    • Disclaimer:

    Please note that all payments are subject to applicable service fees and processing times. The City of Shawnee is not responsible for delays caused by third-party payment providers.
