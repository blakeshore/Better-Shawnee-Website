Bill Pay

The City of Shawnee provides online lookup, review, and payment access to your Shawnee Municipal Authority utility accounts and Municipal Court citations through our gateway. You do not have to make a payment on the site. Review your billing and payment history or your most current outstanding citations

Service Fee:

Effective March 7, 2025 , a 3.75% service fee ($2.50 minimum) will be assessed to all credit/debit card payments. eCheck transactions will incur a service fee of $1.95 (checks cannot be accepted over the telephone)

eCheck transactions will incur a service fee of $1.95 (checks cannot be accepted over the telephone).

    Click to continue to Online Account Access

    You can pay with VISA or Master Card online and view your Utility Payments and Usage History.

    The City does offer other options to pay utility bills without being charged the credit/debit card or eCheck processing fees. The options offered are listed below:

  • Automatic Bank Draft (PDF)

    • If choosing this payment option, the utility bill would draft on the due date each month from a checking account unless that date falls on a weekend or holiday, then it would draft the next business day. Please attach a voided blank check along with submitting the ACH form.

  • Cash Payment in Person.
  • Check Payment in Person or by Mail.
  • Kiosks at Homeland Store at 2705 N Harrison and Shawnee Tag Agency at 301 W Highland (separate service fee).
  • Night-Drop located on the north side of City Hall for checks or money orders.

    • Disclaimer:

    The City of Shawnee makes every effort to ensure that the externally hosted Online Payments system is up to date and accurate.

    If you have any questions or are experiencing online payment problems, please contact:

    • Utility Billing Customer Service: 405-878-1560
    • Municipal Court: 405-878-1624
