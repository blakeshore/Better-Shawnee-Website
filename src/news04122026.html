On April 12, 2026, the City of Shawnee hosted a community cleanup event at the historic Santa Fe Depot. Volunteers from across the city came together to help clean and improve the area, showing strong community support for preserving local landmarks.

Participants spent the day picking up trash, clearing debris, and helping maintain the grounds around the depot. The event was organized to keep the space clean and welcoming for visitors while also protecting an important piece of Shawnee’s history.

City officials and organizers expressed appreciation for everyone who volunteered their time. Events like this highlight how community involvement can make a visible difference in maintaining public spaces.

Residents are encouraged to stay involved in future cleanup events and other local activities that support the upkeep and improvement of the city.

Pick up at Recreation Center, 1234 N. ABC, The center is about on Monday to Friday form 9:00AM to 5:00PM

For more information, visit the City's Special events page.
