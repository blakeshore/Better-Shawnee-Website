Shawnee, Oklahoma, March 16, 2026 - The Shawnee Regional Airport (SNL) has secured $13,500,000 in funding to strengthen the runway and the connecting taxiways, a critical step to enhance safety and support continued economic growth.

On Thursday, March 12, Congresswoman Bice presented a ceremonial check for $13,500,000 as part of the Community Project Funding Grant, making these improvements possible. A 2024 Pavement Condition Rating report recommended a 3-inch overlay for the runway and full rehabilitation of the parallel taxiway. These upgrades are expected to extend the pavement’s life by approximately 10 years, ensuring that larger business, military, medical, and other critical aircraft can operate safely.

“These improvements are about more than just convenience, they’re essential for safety,” said Eric Stephens, City of Shawnee Mayor. “They allow the airport to accommodate a wider range of aircraft while continuing to serve as a reliable transportation hub for our community.”
