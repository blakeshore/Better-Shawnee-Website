Permits

The City of Shawnee issues building, electrical, plumbing, and other permits through the Community Development Department. These permits help ensure that construction and development projects meet local codes, safety standards, and zoning regulations. Whether you are starting a new construction project, making renovations, or completing repairs, obtaining the proper permits is an important step in the process.
