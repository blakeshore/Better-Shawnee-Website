Downtown Shawnee Summer Festival

Time • April 17th, 2026 at 5:00 PM

The annual Downtown Shawnee Summer Festival will feature live music, local vendors, and family-friendly activities.

Come celebrate the start of summer with us at the Downtown Shawnee Summer Festival! Enjoy live music from local bands, browse a variety of vendors offering unique products and delicious food, and participate in fun activities for all ages. The festival will take place in the heart of downtown Shawnee from 5:00 PM to 10:00pm.

Thunder Watch Party

Time • May 19, 2026 at 6:00 PM

Join us for an exciting night of watching the OKC Thunder play in the NBA Playoffs! Featuring special guests and prizes.

Come cheer on the OKC Thunder as they continue their run for an NBA Championship! The night includes exciting basketball action, special guest appearnaces, and chances to win prizes. Starting at 6:00 PM, the event will be held at the Shawnee Community Center and last until the end of the game.

Community Cleanup Day

Time • June 14, 2026 at 9:00 AM

Help us keep our community clean and beautiful! From 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM, volunteers are welcome to participate and make a difference for their community.

Help make an impact in your community by volunteering for our Community Cleanup Day! This event is a great opportunity to give back and help keep our city clean. Volunteers can meet at the city park at 9:00 AM, where we will be organizing the cleanup efforts and providing necessary supplies.

Shawnee Farmers Market

Time • July 12, 2026 at 8:00 AM

Support local farmers and enjoy fresh, seasonal produce!

Come visit the Shawnee Farmers Market and give back to the community! There will be vendors offering fresh, locally grown produce. Want to host your own stand at the market? Contact us to learn more!

The event will start at 8:00 AM and run until 2:00 PM. The market will be held in the parking lot of the Shawnee Community Center.
